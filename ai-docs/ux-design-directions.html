Schultz Hockey League – Design Directions

Blended layout concepts: Command Center density, Draft Day Live clarity, and Schedule/Standings navigation. Mobile-first with dark slate theme, teal primary and periwinkle secondary accents, and high-contrast states.

Direction 1 · Draft Day Live
  • Two-pane desktop: board + context; current pick banner pinned.
  • Mobile: stacked cards; quick actions stay reachable.
  • High-contrast states for current pick, keepers, conflicts.
Direction 2 · Command Center
  • Desktop rail + sticky header; quick KPI cards.
  • Dense tables for standings/schedules with filters.
  • Action shortcuts for admins (publish, approve) in header.
Direction 3 · Schedules & Standings Hub
  • Tabbed views: schedule / standings / stats.
  • Filter chips; responsive tables → summary tiles on mobile.
  • Sticky league/season/team context; deep-linkable filters.
Recommended blend: use Direction 1 for draft flow prominence, Direction 2 for desktop density/rail, and Direction 3 for tabbed schedule/standings navigation. Apply mint accents for active/safe states and amber/red for pending/errors. Keep keyboard focus rings strong and consistent.
